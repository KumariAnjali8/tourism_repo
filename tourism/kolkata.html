Kolkata

Kolkata known as the “City of Joy” is located on the banks of the Hooghly and is one of the biggest cities in India. The city has a rich history and was the capital of India till 1912. Kolkata is a vibrant and diverse city with a rich history, culture, and cuisine. It is one of the few cities which has remnants of British architecture and planning providing the visitors a glimpse of the era gone
